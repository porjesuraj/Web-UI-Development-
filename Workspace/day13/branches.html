Pune - Market Yard

A library is an integral part of any educational institution. SunBeam Students have long enjoyed the educational and cultural benefits of our library service. Library resources and usage has grown steadily, and today the library at SunBeam Institute of Information Technology is fully equipped with over 100 titles and periodicals. The library currently offers a collection of hundreds of items, including books, magazines, CDs, and more which are required to fulfill needs of a candidate undergoing a CDAC ACTS IT course.

It houses books suggested from C-DAC and many other popular titles which help the students to gain additional knowledge in areas of their interest.

The library facility at SunBeam offers increased convenience in skill building, which in turn facilitates increased enthusiastic participation of our students in interactive academic sessions.

Referencing varied study material from our library has helped instill self-confidence in our students and they are known to be in greater comfort in asking relative critical questions during the theory & practical sequences. All our students who have been accredited with International certifications have found our library to be a boon in terms of easy accessibility to vast reference material.Referencing varied study material from our library has helped instill self-confidence in our students and they are known to be in greater comfort in asking relative critical questions during the theory & practical sequences.
